SAN DIEGO

WHAT I DID

My Facorite vacation is when I went to San Diego. While in San Diego, I spent a day at SeaWorld and got to go on some fire rides. The park had a good mix of things to do, making sure we werent getting bored. I also visited the San Diego Zoo, which had a ton of interesting animals and well-designed exhibits. Both places were great to walk around and made the trip feel worthwhile.

LOCATION & FACTS

  • Located in Southern California near the U.S. Mexico border
  • Population of around 1.4 million
  • Known for mild, sunny weather year-round
  • Home to the San Diego Zoo and SeaWorld
  • Over 70 miles of coastline with popular beaches
  • Contains Balboa Park with museums and gardens
  • Major U.S. Navy and Marine Corps presence
  • Home to UC San Diego (UCSD)
  • Borders the city of Tijuana, Mexico
